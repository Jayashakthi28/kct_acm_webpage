ABOUT US

ABOUT KCT

Kumaraguru College of Technology(KCT),coimbatore was started in 1984.It is well known for its nature and discipline.KCT has been accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation council(NAAC) with Grade 'A'.And all the UG courses have also been accredited by National Board of Accreditation(NBA).KCT provides a quality education which helps students to bloom throughout their life not only in education,as well as economic and social development of India.It provides numerous opportunities for students as well as faculties to shine like a star in the world of technology.The college pursues excellence in providing training to develop a sense of professional responsibilty,social and cultural awareness and set students on the path to leadership.

ABOUT ACM

ACM-"Association For Computer Machinery" founded in 1947,is the world's biggest network ,which has impacted over thousands of young minds and assist them with diverse possibilities to uplift their skills. It supports the professional growth of its members for a life-long learning,career development and professional working.Benefits of being an ACM member is,it provides unique volunteering opportunities to gain hands-on experience and Knowledge which enables to form the destiny of computing.

ABOUT KCT IT STUDENT CHAPTER

KCT IT student chapter is a "Coding Community" started, to light candles in and each and every member of KUMARAGURU establishments. We aim to combine young minds together and strive towards our goal in Technical and as well as Non- Technical. This chapter was started in the year 2021 with the vision to enhance our skills and innovation amongst the students.we tend to provide you the BEST out of all.
